Snap Rewards Affiliate Program

Earn recurring revenue by referring Shopify merchants to Snap Rewards, the app that helps convert in-store customers into online buyers through receipt-based discount campaigns.

Why Join?

  • 30% recurring commission on every paying merchant you refer
  • Earn for up to 12 months per referral
  • No cap on earnings
  • Payouts via bank transfer or PayPal

Who Should Join?

The affiliate program is ideal for:

  • Shopify developers and freelancers
  • YouTube creators, bloggers, and educators
  • Shopify app reviewers and tutorial creators
  • Agencies and consultants working with retail or omnichannel brands
  • Existing Snap Rewards users

How It Works

  1. Email us at ben@snap-rewards.com with a short introduction
  2. We will issue you a unique referral code or tracking link
  3. Share the link through your content, direct messages, or client work
  4. Get paid monthly as referred merchants install and subscribe

Cookie Duration

The referral cookie is valid for 30 days. Merchants must install the app within that period to count as a referral.

Payouts

  • Paid monthly via bank transfer or PayPal
  • Minimum payout amount is $50
  • Payouts begin once referred merchants are billed

Apply Now

Email ben@snap-rewards.com to apply. Please include:

  • Your name or company
  • A brief summary of how you plan to promote Snap Rewards
  • Links to your content, portfolio, or relevant audience

We will respond within 1 to 2 business days with your referral code and onboarding instructions.
