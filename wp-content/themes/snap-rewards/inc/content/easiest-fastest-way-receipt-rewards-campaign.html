How to Launch a Receipt Rewards Campaign Without Developers

Most businesses assume they need developers, custom systems, and months of setup. That’s expensive and slow. With Shopify and Snap Rewards, you roll out a working program in hours.

Here’s the flow:

  • Customer buys in-store.
  • They upload their receipt on your branded rewards page.
  • Snap Rewards automatically verifies the receipt.
  • Rewards are issued: discounts, prize entries, or free samples.
  • Customer details (including emails) appear in your Shopify dashboard.

Can You Use Snap Rewards Without Shopify?

Yes. If your main store isn’t on Shopify, you can still run Snap Rewards. Spin up a simple Shopify landing page, connect the app, and send customers there. They upload receipts, claim rewards, and you collect the same data – no migration needed.
