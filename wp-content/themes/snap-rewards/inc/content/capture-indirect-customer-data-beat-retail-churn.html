What Data is Collected from a Snap Receipt?

SKU-Level Detail: Exactly which products, brands, and quantities were purchased.
Basket Composition: What items are typically bought together (e.g., a customer buying diapers also buys coffee).

Transaction Context: The date, time, and specific store location of the purchase.
Financials: Individual item prices, total spend, taxes, and discounts applied.
